Charity

This year again Battle Royale is giving back by donating most of all the profits from the event to Child's Play Charity. Child's Play is a community based charity grown and nurtured from the game culture and industry. They have raised over 7 million dollars in donations of toys, games, books and cash for sick kids in children's hospitals across North America and the world have been collected since our inception. We at Battle Royale love to give back to the community, espicially those less fortunate. Child's Play has been a big supporter of Battle Royale, and we thank them.
