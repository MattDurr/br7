Charity

This year again Battle Royale is giving back by donating most or all of the profits from the event to Child's Play Charity. Child's Play is a community-based charity grown and nurtured from the game culture and industry. They have raised over 7 million dollars in donations of toys, games, books and cash for sick kids in children's hospitals across North America and the world. We at Battle Royale love to give back to the community, especially those less fortunate. Child's Play has been a big supporter of Battle Royale, and we thank them.
