Announcing Battle Royale VII!
October 30, 2013
We are happy to announce that Battle Royale VII will be taking place on February 22nd to 23rd, 2014! This 24 hour LAN party will be held in the prestigious Tabaret Hall at the University of Ottawa.

As in past years, we aimed to hold the event at the beginning of November. However, after a series of unfortunate events, we were not able to book our usual venue. As a result, we broadened our search for other venues, and were excited to book Tabaret Hall in late February.

Ticket sales will begin in early January. The exact date will be announced as we draw closer to the event. We hope to see you all at Ottawa's Largest Gaming Lan Party! Follow us on Twitter and check out our website for the latest updates.
